

# Sunanda Entertainment Limited - Corporate Website Plan

## Overview
A high-end, modern corporate website that positions Sunanda Entertainment Limited as a strategic growth partner for businesses. The design will be sophisticated, minimal yet bold, with a premium feel that communicates confidence, intelligence, and creativity.

---

## Visual Identity

### Color Palette
- **Primary**: Dark charcoal (#1A1A1A) for backgrounds and depth
- **Secondary**: Soft white/off-white (#F8F8F6) for clean contrast
- **Accent**: Gold/Amber (#C9A962) for premium highlights and CTAs
- **Text**: High-contrast typography for readability

### Typography
- Premium sans-serif for headlines (strong, confident hierarchy)
- Clean readable body font for content
- Large, impactful headlines with generous letter-spacing

### Design Elements
- Generous whitespace and modern grid layouts
- Subtle gradient overlays on dark sections
- Elegant dividers and section transitions
- Sophisticated hover states on interactive elements

---

## Website Structure

### 1. Home / Hero Section
- Full-width cinematic hero with dark overlay
- Bold headline: "Where Strategy Meets Creativity to Build Powerful Brands"
- Supporting subtext highlighting integrated marketing expertise
- Dual CTAs: "Start a Project" (primary gold) | "Explore Services" (outline)
- Animated service highlights scrolling or fading in
- Subtle scroll indicator

### 2. Who We Are
- Clear brand positioning as full-service marketing partner
- Mission, Vision, and Values displayed in elegant cards
- Emphasis on trust, innovation, and measurable impact
- Optional team leadership section placeholder
- Visual storytelling with imagery placeholders

### 3. What We Do - Services Section
Six service cards with individual focus on business outcomes:
- **Print Media Solutions** - Traditional excellence meets modern strategy
- **Digital Marketing & Performance Campaigns** - Data-driven growth
- **Outdoor & Transit Advertising** - Maximum visibility, lasting impressions
- **Electronic & Broadcast Media** - Engaging audiences at scale
- **Creative & Brand Strategy** - Compelling narratives that convert
- **Promotional & Activation Campaigns** - Direct engagement, measurable ROI

Each card highlights business outcomes and ROI focus, not just features.

### 4. Our Approach
A four-step visual process flow:
1. **Research-Driven Planning** - Deep market and audience insights
2. **Creative Ideation** - Strategy meets imagination
3. **Technology-Enabled Execution** - Precision delivery at scale
4. **Performance Tracking & Optimization** - Continuous improvement

Presented as a horizontal timeline or stepped cards with elegant transitions.

### 5. Why Sunanda Entertainment
Key differentiators displayed prominently:
- Integrated multi-channel campaigns
- Strategic thinking backed by execution strength
- Transparent processes
- Long-term client partnerships
- Consistent quality and accountability

Presented with icons and concise value statements.

### 6. Placeholder Sections
- **Case Studies**: Grid layout ready for future content
- **Testimonials**: Carousel/card format for client quotes
- **Portfolio**: Gallery grid with hover effects

### 7. Contact Section
- Clean, conversion-focused layout
- Professional contact form (visual only for now):
  - Name, Email, Phone
  - Company Name
  - Service Interest dropdown
  - Project Brief textarea
- Gold accent submit button
- Direct contact information displayed alongside

### 8. Footer
- Brand logo and tagline
- Navigation links
- Social media placeholders
- Copyright and professional tone

---

## Animations & Interactions (Elegant & Subtle)

- **Fade-in on scroll** for sections and content blocks
- **Smooth hover effects** on cards, buttons, and links
- **Subtle scale animations** on service cards
- **Underline animations** on navigation links
- **Smooth page transitions** between sections
- **Parallax-lite** subtle background movement on hero

---

## Technical Implementation

- **SEO-optimized** semantic HTML structure
- **Mobile-first** responsive design (all breakpoints)
- **Fast loading** optimized assets and code
- **Accessibility** proper contrast, focus states, ARIA labels
- **Smooth scrolling** navigation to sections

---

## Pages & Navigation

Single-page scrolling experience with anchor navigation:
- Home (Hero)
- Who We Are
- What We Do (Services)
- Our Approach
- Why Us
- Contact

Sticky header with navigation that highlights current section on scroll.

---

## Summary

This website will establish Sunanda Entertainment Limited as a premium, enterprise-ready marketing and advertising agency. The dark, sophisticated aesthetic combined with gold accents will convey authority and creativity, while the clear service positioning will communicate the value of partnering with your agency for long-term business growth.
